Creations by Mandy

Mandy Schulien is currently a freshman at The Cleveland Institute of Art. She is planning to study Illustration, and explore other majors and medias through electives. She is from Frederick, Maryland, and previously attended Oakdale High School.

Mandy has expirience with Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, along with traditional art mediums such as Oil paint, Chalk Pastel, Graphite, Charcoal, ect. SHe is very grateful she is able to broaden her horizons at CIA and hope to grow more skills to benefit her future career.
